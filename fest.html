NAURYZ FESTIVAL AT BAYAN-ULGII PROVINCE

The Kazakh New Year’s celebration of Nauryz has been
celebrated by Turkic and Iranian people for over 2000 years.
It is the biggest holiday in the Kazakh year. The day
corresponds to the spring equinox
