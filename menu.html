INTERNATIONAL FLIGHTS MONGOLIA

Chinggis Khaan International Airport outside of Ulaanbaatar (located just 18
km to the southwest of Ulaanbaatar) is the only airport in Mongolia that offers
international flights. Direct flights are available from Berlin (German), Moscow
(Russia), Beijing (China), Seoul (Korea), and Tokyo (Japan).
